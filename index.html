About Me

Welcome to my website! I'm Mahinur Rahman Pamel, a passionate Computer Science and Engineering student currently in my third year at Metropolitan University, Sylhet, Bangladesh. Beyond academics, I actively engage in leadership and community activities. As the President of Knocking Collaborator’s Youth Foundation, I lead initiatives in public speaking, leadership, and charity. I am also the Secretary (Training) for the Metropolitan University Rover Scout Group, where I guide a team in scouting and leadership development. Additionally, I founded "Call for Blood," an online blood donation platform to help connect donors and recipients in Sylhet. My experiences have been recognized with prestigious awards such as the President’s Scout Award, which is the highest honor in Bangladesh Scouts, and the Study in India (SII) Scholarship, funded by the Government of India. I’ve also attended various workshops and training programs focused on leadership and research. Professionally, I'm co-founded Chitrachar, a photography venture, and i also worked as a Social Media Manager for Wesitely LTD, enhancing its online presence. I look forward to connecting with you and sharing more about my work and experiences. Thank you for visiting!
